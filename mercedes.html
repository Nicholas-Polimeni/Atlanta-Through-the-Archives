Mercedes-Benz Superdome Construction

Summary

The Mercedes-Benz stadium’s construction began in 2014, as a virtually direct replacement of the Georgia Dome, a sports stadium completed in 1992 and demolished in 2017. Researchers have commented that the destruction of Gold Dome was justified, as the site and the neighboring conference center were a barrier to intown opportunities.
