Northside Public Housing (3601 Piedmont Road)

Summary

The Atlanta Housing Authority receives a 6.7 million dollar grant for the construction of a 209-unit highrise for the elderly. The site was successfully rezoned, despite continuing local movements to halt the development of public housing in Northside.
