HUD Study Published on Empowerment Zones

Summary

The US Department of Housing and Urban Development publishes an inconclusive study on the connection between EZ and EC programs and overall community growth. While specific businesses that are given tax breaks or investments experienced benefit at the time, there was not strong enough data to show that the benefits were widespread enough to pull the entire community zone up.
