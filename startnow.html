Start Now Atlanta Week

Summary

"Start Now Atlanta Week" backed by religious organizations in Atlanta raise awareness for the 160,000 people (82% African American) living in 40,000 unfit dwellings throughout the city, highlighting a major division in housing and health quality between racial and class groups.
